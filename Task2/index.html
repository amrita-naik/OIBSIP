Steve Jobs

1955-2011

Steve Jobs was one of the most influential entrepreneurs in the field of IT.

He was the founder and CEO of Apple Inc. - one of the biggest IT companies in the world. His ideas and passion for innovative inventions is celebrated by the mordern IT community. His vision and drive have forever changed computing by bringing us the Mac, the iPhone and the iPad. He achieved a lot at a very young age and inspired an entire generation of young entrepreneurs.

"Your work is going to fill a large part of your life, and the only way to be truly satisfied is to do what you believe is great work. And the only way to do great work is to love what you do. If you haven't found it yet, keep looking. Don't settle. As with all matters of the heart, you'll know when you find it."

Steve Jobs, CEO, Apple Inc.
